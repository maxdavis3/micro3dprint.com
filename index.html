Capability: Channels 50-200 µm, precise geometries

Applications: Lab-on-chip devices, diagnostic systems, drug delivery

Integrated microfluidic components with serpentine channels, mixing chambers, and reaction vessels. Traditional manufacturing requires multiple bonded layers. DMLS produces monolithic structures with perfect internal geometry.

Micro-Precision Manufacturing Process

1. Design & Optimization

CAD files reviewed for ultra-precision manufacturing. Layout optimized for batch nesting (100+ parts per build). Tolerances validated achievable. Support strategy designed for minimal removal.

2. Fine Layer DMLS

Layer thickness reduced to 0.025-0.050mm for enhanced detail. Laser parameters optimized for precision. Build time longer but surface finish superior. 99.5%+ density achieved.

3. Post-Processing

Support removal via micro-machining. Surface polishing to Ra 0.2-0.8 µm. CNC finishing for ±0.001" tolerance features. Dimensional verification via CMM or SEM.

4. Quality Verification

100% inspection of critical dimensions. SEM analysis for feature detail. Functional testing as applicable. Certification for medical/aerospace applications.

Micro-Precision Case Studies

Microfluidic Diagnostic Device

Challenge: Lab-on-chip device required integrated microfluidic channels (100 µm) with precision serpentine mixers. Traditional bonded layer approach impractical.

Solution: Monolithic DMLS manufacturing with 0.004" minimum channels. 100+ units per build.

Result: $2/unit manufacturing cost vs $50+ traditional. 6-week lead time vs 16 weeks.

Implantable Cardiac Device

Challenge: Ultra-compact titanium housing for pacemaker required ±0.001" tolerances and biocompatible surface.

Solution: Fine-layer DMLS Ti6Al4V with 0.004" wall thickness. Integrated features for internal electronics mounting.

Result: 15% smaller device volume. 100% FDA biocompatibility verified.

Luxury Watchmaking Components

Challenge: Mechanical watch escape wheel required 0.002" precision with complex geometry for improved accuracy.

Solution: Gold DMLS with ultra-fine layer control. Integrated jewel settings.

Result: Chronometer-grade accuracy. Collectors' item value enhanced through manufacturing innovation.
