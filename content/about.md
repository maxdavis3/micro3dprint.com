## Precision Metal Printing at the Micro Scale

Micro 3D Print covers the precision ceiling of metal additive manufacturing — DMLS parameter
sets optimized for 50 µm layers, 80 µm laser spot diameter, and feature resolution
approaching 0.15 mm (0.006 inch). Where standard DMLS optimizes for build rate and part
size, micro-DMLS optimizes for accuracy and minimum feature size.

Applications: watchmaking (movement bridges, crown components), surgical instruments
(laparoscopic tips, implantable device housings), microfluidics (stainless channels
≥ 0.3 mm), precision sensors, and miniaturized electronics housings.

Standard DMLS tolerances are ±0.003 inch + ±0.001 inch per inch accumulated.
Micro-scale parameters achieve ±0.001 inch on small features — at the cost of
build rate (25-50% of standard speed).

Published by Ben Stagl and Max Davis as part of the [Metal 3D Printing Network](https://dmls3d.com).
